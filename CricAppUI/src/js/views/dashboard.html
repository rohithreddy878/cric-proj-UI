Cricket

Wikipedia defines Cricket as a 'bat-and-ball' game that is played between two teams of eleven players. Funnily, it is a bat-vs-ball game in its essence, isn't it? Batsmen look to hit the ball and run, Bowlers look to hit the wickets. The English Elite that created the game in the 16th century made it that - for the Elite. And it remained a sport for the elite for centuries. Then the English decided to play it in the Commonwealth nations, where it grew as a 'Sport played to beat the "cruel British," at their own game'. Lo and behold, a couple of centuries later, we are playing a sport where the batsmen look to hit the ball outside the ropes and Bowlers look to evade the hitting range.
